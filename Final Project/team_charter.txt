PROJECT MISSION STATEMENT
--------------------------
Our mission is to design, develop, and validate an automated cybersecurity
pipeline that detects credential-based attacks in Windows Security event logs,
reduces manual analyst workload, and produces actionable threat intelligence
reports in compliance with NIST SP 800-53, GDPR, and HIPAA requirements.


TEAM GOALS
----------
1. Deliver working, tested automation scripts (PowerShell and Python) by the
   final project deadline.
2. Produce a professional written report and presentation that accurately
   reflect the technical work completed.
3. Maintain clear, respectful communication throughout the project.
4. Ensure all code and documentation meets ethical and compliance standards.
5. Submit all deliverables on time with both team members contributing equally.

DECISION-MAKING POLICY
-----------------------
Decisions will be made by mutual agreement. If the team cannot reach consensus
on a technical or creative direction within one discussion session, the team
member whose primary area of responsibility the decision falls under will make
the final call. All significant decisions will be logged in our shared notes.


CONFLICT RESOLUTION
-------------------
1. Raise the concern directly and respectfully in a private team message first.
2. If unresolved within 48 hours, bring the issue to a team meeting for open
   discussion.
3. If still unresolved, escalate to the course instructor for guidance.
We commit to addressing disagreements professionally and without personal
criticism.


ACADEMIC INTEGRITY COMMITMENT
------------------------------
All team members agree to uphold the academic honesty policies of the
University of Arizona Global Campus. All submitted work will be original, and
any external sources used will be properly cited in APA format. Team members
will not misrepresent individual contributions.


PARTICIPATION AND ACCOUNTABILITY
---------------------------------
If a team member is unable to complete an assigned task by the agreed internal
deadline, they will notify the team at least 48 hours in advance so
responsibilities can be redistributed without jeopardizing the submission
deadline.
